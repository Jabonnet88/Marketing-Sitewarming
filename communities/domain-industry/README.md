# Community — Domain Industry

## What this community is
Domain investors, brokers, registrars, and domain industry professionals who buy, sell, develop, and discuss premium domains. This is SiteWarming's home turf.

## Why SiteWarming engages here
Credibility. Being a consistent, thoughtful voice in the domain industry community establishes SiteWarming as a serious player — not just a new platform, but a brand that understands the industry deeply. Domain investors are also potential customers, partners, and referrers.
