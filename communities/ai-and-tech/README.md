# Community — AI & Tech

## What this community is
Tech influencers, AI researchers, journalists, and thought leaders who drive the conversation around artificial intelligence, emerging technology, and the future of work. Posts here often go viral and get thousands of impressions.

## Why SiteWarming engages here
High-reach posts = high-reach comments. A strong comment on a post by @a16z or @DeryaTR_ can get hundreds of impressions without any paid promotion. It also positions SiteWarming as a company that understands technology — not just a domain registrar.

## Types of posts to engage with
- AI capability announcements (new models, benchmarks, milestones)
- Future of work — jobs, automation, identity
- AI and creativity/art debates
- Enterprise AI adoption stories
- Hot takes that deserve a DATA or CONTRARIAN response

## Engagement strategy
- DATA comments perform exceptionally well here — cite specific stats or trends
- CONTRARIAN works well if you can genuinely push back, not just for the sake of it
- Keep comments analytical — this audience is smart and spots fluff instantly
- Aim for comments that reframe the original insight at a deeper level
