# Community — VC & Investors

## What this community is
Venture capitalists, angel investors, and investment firms who share takes on markets, company building, AI, and the future. They set the narrative for what's hot and what's not in the startup world.

## Why SiteWarming engages here
VCs influence founders. When a VC tweets about AI infrastructure, domain strategy, or digital assets, their followers are the exact people who will eventually need premium domains. Engaging here builds top-of-funnel brand awareness with high-influence accounts.

## Key accounts to watch
- @a16z — Andreessen Horowitz, major AI and tech investor
- @jasonlk — SaaStr, SaaS investor, AI in business operations
- @andrewchen — growth, consumer products, network effects
- @auren — SafeGraph CEO, data and investment angles

## Types of posts to engage with
- Macro takes on AI and the economy
- Predictions about which sectors will win/lose
- Portfolio company stories and lessons
- Contrarian investment theses
- AI replacing roles / restructuring teams

## Engagement strategy
- Be sharp and concise — VCs move fast and value clarity
- DATA comments are the most credible here
- Avoid fluff or enthusiasm — analytical tone only
- CONTRARIAN can work well but must be genuinely substantive, not just contrarian for show
