# Community — Startup Founders

## What this community is
Entrepreneurs at various stages — from solopreneurs and early-stage founders to scaling operators. They post about business strategy, team building, product decisions, fundraising, and the mindset of building companies.

## Why SiteWarming engages here
Founders make domain decisions. Whether they're naming a new product, acquiring a premium domain for a rebrand, or warming up a domain before launch, this is the audience with both the need and the budget.

## Types of posts to engage with
- "What I wish I knew before building X"
- Contrarian takes on startup advice (great for CONTRARIAN comments)
- Delegation, team structure, and scaling ops
- Failures and pivots — very authentic, high engagement
- Hot takes on how AI is changing company building

## Engagement strategy
- CONTRARIAN and AFFIRMATION with expansion work best here
- Founders respond well to comments that acknowledge their point then add a layer of nuance
- Avoid being preachy — match their directness
- Best time to engage: within the first 30–60 minutes of a post going live
